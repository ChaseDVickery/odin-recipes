Tofu and Cabbage Stew

This is some kind of tofu and cabbage stew that I modified (slightly) from a tofu and kimchi recipe because I'm lazy and couldn't easily get kimchi for cheap. Hard to go wrong with this one, too- just keep adding stuff in over time and you should be good to go.

Ingredients

  • Vegatable oil
  • 6+ green onions
  • 4 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp gochujang
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • Kimchi (preferred) or some cabbage (pre-sliced coleslaw mix works well, too)
  • 1 block of tofu
  • Mushrooms (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot on high, then add the white parts of the onion, garlic, and ginger for about 3 minutes.
  2. Add broth, cabbage, soy sauce, and gochujang. Mix it in.
  3. Get it to a simmer and cook about 10-15 minutes.
  4. Add tofu and mushrooms and cook another 5-10 minutes.
  5. Sprinkle leftover green onions on top.
  6. Serve on rice if you need it to last, or top it with a fried egg.
